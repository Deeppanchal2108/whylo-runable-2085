# Whylo Design System

## Brand
- **Name:** Whylo
- **Tagline:** Your reviews. Your site. Instantly.

## Colors
- **Background:** `#0b0a09` (near-black, warm)
- **Surface:** `#141210` (cards, elevated elements)
- **Border:** `#2a2420` (subtle warm borders)
- **Accent:** `#e8a87c` (warm amber/orange — primary CTA, highlights)
- **Accent dim:** `#c4845a` (hover states)
- **Text primary:** `#f5f0eb` (warm white)
- **Text secondary:** `#8a7d72` (muted warm gray)
- **Text muted:** `#4a3f38` (very muted)

## Typography
- **Display/Headings:** Instrument Serif (Google Fonts) — italic for emphasis words
- **Body/UI:** Inter — clean, functional
- **Sizes:** Hero 72-96px, Section heads 48-64px, Sub 24px, Body 16px

## Texture
- Dot grid overlay on hero (very subtle, warm tint)
- Slight noise/grain on backgrounds
- No harsh shadows — warm glows only

## Layout
- Full-width sections
- Generous padding (120px vertical)
- Max content width: 1200px centered
- Asymmetric hero layout

## Motion
- Staggered fade-up on page load
- Smooth hover transitions (200ms)
- No jarring animations

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped, amber fill for primary, outlined for secondary
- Cards: `#141210` bg, `1px` warm border, subtle hover glow
- Input: dark fill, amber focus ring
